Clarity for Careers. Confidence for Life.

Clario is a next-generation career guidance and mentorship platform designed to help students and professionals make informed, future-ready career decisions. Through personalized counselling, psychometric assessments, academic planning, and skill-based mentoring, Clario transforms confusion into clarity and aspirations into actionable pathways. We bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by combining expert guidance with emerging industry insights across technology, research, entrepreneurship, design, and global careers. Whether choosing a stream, college, profession, or career transition, Clario empowers individuals to discover their strengths, unlock potential, and confidently shape successful careers in an evolving world.

About Founder

Dr. Pallavi Khatri

Dr. Pallavi Khatri is an academician, researcher, mentor, and emerging career counsellor with extensive experience in higher education, research leadership, and student mentoring. She has guided and mentored students and scholars across diverse academic and career pathways in technology, research, innovation, and emerging interdisciplinary domains. With expertise spanning career guidance, skill mapping, academic mentoring, research planning, and future-readiness counselling, Dr. Khatri combines analytical insight with a student-centric approach to help learners make informed career decisions. Her strong background in Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, IoT, and emerging technologies enables her to guide students toward industry-aligned and globally relevant career opportunities. Dr. Khatri is also associated with faculty development, innovation ecosystems, entrepreneurship initiatives, and research mentoring, giving her a holistic understanding of evolving education and employment landscapes. She believes career counselling is not merely about selecting a profession, but about helping individuals discover their strengths, align passion with purpose, and build meaningful, future-ready careers. Through Clario, she aims to empower students with clarity, confidence, and strategic direction for academic and professional success.
